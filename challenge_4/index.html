Humble Bundle

Humble Bundle is an organization that sells bundles of games together at a cheap price while simultaneously giving proceeds to various charities for giving to the homeless, to impoverished countries, and various other goals and organizations based on the bundle's theme. You can even specify how much you want your proceeds to go to Humble Bundle, the creators of the game, and the charities. There is also a storefront where every purchase on the platform contributes to charity.
